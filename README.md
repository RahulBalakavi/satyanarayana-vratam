# Sri Satyanarayana Vratam — A Guided Course

A self-contained, narrated slide-deck that walks a devotee through the Sri Satyanarayana
Swamy Vratam step by step — the Shodasopachara puja and the five-chapter Katha — with each
mantra in Devanagari + romanization, a play-along reciter (Telugu audio + karaoke
transcript), the stories narrated in Telugu with English read-along, and a semantic
background image per phase.

**Live:** https://rahulbalakavi.github.io/satyanarayana-vratam/

Mantras are traditional liturgy (transcribed from public repositories: vignanam.org,
stotranidhi.com, drikpanchang.com). Story narration is an original retelling of the
traditional Skanda-Purana katha. Images are Creative-Commons / public-domain from
Wikimedia Commons, attributed on each slide. Recitation & narration voice: Sarvam AI.
